Our Services










At Pup&Jam, we offer pajama clothing for all breeds of dog. Whether your best friend is a fierce chihuahua or a mighty great dane, we have jammies to fit all shapes and sizes!


And best of all, whenever you buy a pair for your own pup, folks at Pup&Jam will match your purchase by donating the same pajama set to an animal shelter in the area. It's a fantastic way to spoil your furry friend, look out for others, and feel good in the duality of giving and receiving.




Because of our philosophy around benefaction, our made-with-love clothing can cost more than what you might otherwise see in a pet store. We structure our pricing this way so that we can cover the production costs of two items, as each purchase is matched with an identical donation. We truly hope that you will join us in the spirit of sharing, and we thank you for your generosity and compassion!
