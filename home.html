Our Mission Statement

            Here at Pup&Jam, we know that furry friends are family. And as a family unit, it's our duty to keep each other warm, safe, and full. With compassion and care at the heart of everything we do, Pup&Jam produces uniquely adorable dog clothing, so you and your best friend can snuggle up in jammies together.

            And to further our mission of spreading the love, any pajama purchase you make will be matched with an identical set, the latter of which will then be donated to a local animal shelter. We really do believe that affection is best when there's plenty to go around, and what could be better than showing your pup you care by keeping them and others cozy?









Thank you for visiting us! We're so glad you stopped by. Be sure to come back soon!
